Team Managers

Activity 1a

Scenario

Imagine your name is Darren and you are a team manager signing your basketball team up for this year’s Pesta Sukan competition. You and your team attended last year's Pesta Sukan competition and you just received an email that this year's Pesta Sukan registration has begun.

Activity

Sign up for the Mens Open Basketball competition for your team from the email.

Activity 1b

Activity

Fill in 2 team members information on your own.

Activity 1c

Scenario

Imagine you'd decided to send your team members a link to fill in their own information instead.

Activity

Go back to the team page and try that path. You don't need to actually send anyone anything, just walk through what you'd do.

Activity 2 (Mobile)

Scenario

Imagine now on the flip side, you are a participant and your team manager has signed your team up for a Pesta Sukan event and sent you this link to complete your part.

Activity

Log in to GMS to complete your registration.

Activity 3 (Mobile)

Scenario

Imagine you have registered a few teams for different events.

Activity

Find the page to see all the events that you have registered your teams for.
